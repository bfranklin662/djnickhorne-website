Nick Horne is a 34 year old up-and-coming DJ and music producer originally from Exeter, Devon, now making his mark on the London scene. With over a decade of experience behind the decks, Nick has built a reputation for delivering high-energy sets that span multiple genres, with a strong focus on house and commercial club music blending old-school classics with the latest industry hits with seamless precision. Whether he's lighting up a packed club or working in the studio, Nick Horne delivers sound that’s fresh, vibrant, and undeniably dancefloor-ready.

His talents have taken him international, with performances across two continents - including standout sets in Greece and Dubai. Nick has also shared the stage with some of the biggest names in the industry, including Groove Armada, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, and Freejak. He currently holds a residency at the iconic Timepiece Nightclub in Exeter, where his signature sound continues to ignite the local scene. With an ear for crowd-pleasing beats and a passion for pushing boundaries, Nick Horne is fast becoming a name to watch in the UK and beyond.

As a producer, Nick continues to make waves. His remix of “Lady” by Modjo has surpassed 100,000 streams on Spotify, showcasing his ability to blend nostalgic classics with a fresh, modern twist. His original track “Levitate” is a standout big room house anthem, while other releases such as “I Can Get You” and “Save Me” have earned airplay on BBC radio stations, signaling his growing influence in the UK dance music landscape.
